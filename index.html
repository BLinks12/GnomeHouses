GnomeCoin (GNM)

How It Works

Welcome to GnomeCoin (GNM), the magical currency that fuels the creation of gnome houses! Every time our community reaches a new milestone, new gnome houses are generated through the Midjourney API. Click the button above to discover a new gnome house and join us in expanding the gnome realm!

The Lore of GnomeCoin

Deep within the enchanted forests, mystical gnomes craft their whimsical homes hidden from human eyes. GnomeCoin (GNM) is the essence that brings these creations to life. By embracing GNM, you become part of the magic that allows new gnome abodes to sprout and flourish.
